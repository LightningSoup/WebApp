How Lightning Soup Works


In a nutshell, I guess




1. You make stuff
Easy enough. We're working on a few tools to make it easier for you.








2. You publish your stuff
When you publish, you get:
  • Your stuff's Lightning Soup URL. Share this like a normal website link.
  • Your stuff's IPFS hash. Keep this to recover your stuff in case it gets lost, or pin it to make sure it stays around forever.








3. Others improve on your stuff
Let's face it -- none of us are perfect. That's why anything and everything can be forked and modified. Your sources aare cited for you, and everyone who builds on your work will cite you.








4. Repeat Steps 1-3
Innovation is an ongoing process. Like a cycle, it never ends.
